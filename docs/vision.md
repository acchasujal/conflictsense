## The Vision
ConflictSense is an AI-powered policy contradiction detection engine designed for enterprise environments. It uncovers "structural impossibilities" where distinct organizational policies (e.g., HR, IT Security, Legal) make conflicting claims that cannot simultaneously be satisfied for a given employee population.

## The Goal
To win the Agents League Hackathon by demonstrating a 90-second, highly reliable, visually compelling demo that emphasizes prose reasoning, grounded citations, and human-in-the-loop workflows over sheer agent volume.

## Value Proposition
For the enterprise (represented by synthetic company Nexora Technologies), ConflictSense mitigates regulatory and operational risks by surfacing deep-seated policy contradictions before they result in compliance breaches, backed by auditable evidence.
