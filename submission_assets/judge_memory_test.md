## Ruthless Prioritization
- Keep the anonymity conflict front and center.
- Keep the reasoning trace visible.
- Keep Foundry IQ as the trust anchor.
- Keep the approval gate as the safety anchor.
- Keep the fallback story as a reliability anchor.
- Do not spend judging time on backend mechanics that do not improve trust or memorability.

## Weakening Features
- Anything that looks like a generic chatbot weakens the story.
- Anything that expands the agent list without improving clarity weakens the story.
- Anything that hides the evidence behind dense UI panels weakens the story.
- Anything that makes the demo seem dependent on perfect live inference weakens the story.
