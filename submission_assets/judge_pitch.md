# Judge Pitch

## Opening
ConflictSense finds policy contradictions that hide inside enterprise silos.

## What Makes It Different
- It does not answer questions like a chatbot.
- It does not rely on brute-force keyword search.
- It does not trust the model to invent an answer.
- It uses Azure Foundry IQ to ground every claim in source documents.

## Why It Wins
- **Reasoning:** Judges can watch the thought process unfold in the SSE UI.
- **Reliability:** The system has a 3-tier fallback chain, so the demo finishes even if live inference fails.
- **Responsible AI:** Nothing autonomous happens without human approval.
- **Enterprise Value:** It exposes compliance contradictions before they become regulatory or operational incidents.
- **Memorability:** The anonymity conflict is a simple, sharp story that people remember immediately.

## The One-Sentence Pitch
ConflictSense is an auditable conflict-detection engine for enterprise policy that uses Foundry IQ and human-in-the-loop review to surface structural impossibilities before they become risk.

## The 20-Second Pitch
Enterprises hide contradictions in dozens of policy documents. ConflictSense grounds each document with Foundry IQ, detects conflicts with a reasoning trace, validates them, and then routes the result through a human approval gate. The result is a trustworthy compliance assistant, not another chatbot.

## Closing Line
If policy is the enterprise's source of truth, ConflictSense is the system that catches when the truth contradicts itself.
