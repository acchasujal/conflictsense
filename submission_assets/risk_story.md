# Risk Story

## Why Risk Matters
Enterprise leaders do not only need to know that two policies conflict. They need to know why the conflict matters, who it affects, and what kind of exposure it creates.

RiskQuantifier adds that final leadership layer:
Conflict -> Impact -> Risk

## How Risk Is Grounded
RiskQuantifier only runs after a conflict has passed validation and impact assessment. It uses:
- the validated conflict,
- the grounded citations,
- the impact assessment,
- and explicit uncertainty handling.

It does not invent fines, percentages, employee counts, or legal conclusions that are not supported by source evidence.

## Enterprise Value
The risk layer makes ConflictSense more useful for:
- compliance teams deciding what needs review,
- legal teams assessing contradictory commitments,
- security teams understanding control conflicts,
- executives prioritizing remediation work,
- and auditors tracing claims back to source documents.

## Demo Value
The judge can now see a clean progression:
1. Conflict: The policies cannot both be true.
2. Impact: Real employees, systems, or workflows are affected.
3. Risk: The contradiction creates compliance, legal, governance, operational, reputational, or security exposure.

## Memorable Line
ConflictSense does not stop at finding policy contradictions. It explains why leadership should care.
