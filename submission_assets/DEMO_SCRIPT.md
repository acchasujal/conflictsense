# ConflictSense: 3-Minute Demo Script

**(0:00 - 0:30) The Problem**
"Hello judges, welcome to ConflictSense. Today, enterprise compliance is broken. HR, IT, Legal, and Finance all write their own policies in silos. What happens when these policies fundamentally contradict each other? Employees get caught in the crossfire. We built ConflictSense to autonomously detect these structural contradictions and quantify their human impact."

**(0:30 - 1:15) Employee Accommodation Conflict**
"Let me show you. We’ll run the 'Employee Accommodation Conflict' scenario. Here, our agent ingests the company policies and immediately detects a Critical Contradiction.
*(Point to Conflict Card)*
On one side, HR explicitly guarantees reasonable accommodations for disabled employees. On the other side, IT Security strictly prohibits any non-standard software.
*(Expand Reasoning)*
The result? An employee who needs a screen reader cannot get one. This isn't just bad policy—it's active disability discrimination. Our system mathematically proves this impossibility and recommends a remediation pathway for the CISO and HR."

**(1:15 - 1:45) Whistleblower Protection Conflict**
"Let's look at another one. Whistleblower Protection.
HR promises complete anonymity for anyone reporting harassment. But IT policy dictates that every single action on the corporate network must be logged and tied to a user identity.
If an employee reports abuse, their identity is logged. They are exposed to retaliation. ConflictSense finds this before a lawsuit happens."

**(1:45 - 2:30) Full KB Analysis**
"Now, let's run the flagship feature: The Full Knowledge Base Analysis.
*(Click Full KB Analysis)*
In less than 5 seconds, our Enterprise Agent scans all 7 corporate policies. You can see the live reasoning trace on the right: Ingestion, Cross-Policy Review, Logic Validation, and Human Impact Assessment.
At the top left, the Executive Summary perfectly outlines the risks. We’ve found 5 critical contradictions spanning Data Residency, SaaS Procurement, and BYOD data retention. This is a complete enterprise audit, instantly available."

**(2:30 - 3:00) Upload Analysis**
"What if you are drafting a brand new policy?
Let's upload a drafted 'Remote Work Agreement' alongside our existing 'Export Control' guidelines.
*(Run Upload Analysis)*
ConflictSense uses live LLM reasoning to cross-reference the uploaded drafts. If it finds a contradiction, it surfaces it. If it doesn't, it safely abstains. It never hallucinates."

**(3:00 - 3:30) Accessibility Features & Closing**
"Before we close, notice that ConflictSense is 100% accessible. You can navigate the entire timeline and conflict portfolio using just your keyboard. We’ve implemented strict ARIA labels, semantic HTML, and high-contrast color palettes.
ConflictSense transforms compliance from a bureaucratic checkbox into a human-centered governance platform. Thank you."
