# HR Remote Work Policy — Nexora Technologies
# Department: Human Resources
# Effective: March 2021 (Updated March 2025)
# Classification: INTERNAL

---

## §1. Purpose

Nexora Technologies is committed to supporting flexible working arrangements that enable employees to perform their best work. This policy establishes the framework for remote and distributed work across all Nexora roles.

---

## §2. Eligibility and Location

§2.1 — Employees may work from any global location without prior approval, provided their role does not require physical on-site presence for client-facing or regulated activities.

§2.2 — Employees working internationally for more than 60 consecutive days must notify HR to assess tax and employment law implications. Notification does not constitute a restriction on work location.

§2.3 — HR will not deny a remote work request based on geographic location unless a specific regulatory restriction has been communicated in writing by the Legal & Compliance department.

---

## §3. Equipment and Connectivity

§3.1 — Nexora will provide all employees with company-issued equipment sufficient for their role.

§3.2 — Employees are responsible for maintaining adequate internet connectivity at their chosen work location. A monthly connectivity stipend of £50 is provided.

§3.3 — Employees must connect to the Nexora VPN for all work conducted outside of company premises.

---

## §4. Contractor and Temporary Staff Access

§4.1 — External contractors are subject to the same remote work permissions as permanent employees during their contract term.

§4.2 — External contractors and temporary staff may be granted network access without MFA if approved in writing by their sponsoring department manager. This exception applies only to network segments explicitly designated as low-risk by IT Infrastructure.

---

## §5. Working Hours and Availability

§5.1 — Remote employees are expected to maintain core availability hours of 10:00–16:00 in their team's primary timezone.

§5.2 — Employees in significantly different timezones should agree an overlap window with their line manager.

---

## §6. Data and Security

§6.1 — Remote employees must comply with all provisions of the IT Security Policy when working outside company premises.

§6.2 — Client data, financial records, and HR records must not be accessed from unsecured networks (e.g., public Wi-Fi) without VPN.

---

## §7. Health and Safety

§7.1 — Employees are responsible for ensuring their remote work environment meets basic health and safety requirements. Nexora provides a one-time home office setup allowance of £300.

---

## §8. Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually by HR in consultation with Legal & Compliance and IT Infrastructure.

*Policy Owner: Chief People Officer (CHRO)*
*Review Cycle: Annual*
