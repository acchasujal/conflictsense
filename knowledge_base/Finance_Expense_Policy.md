# Finance & Expense Policy — Nexora Technologies
# Department: Finance
# Effective: June 2024
# Classification: INTERNAL

---

## §1. Purpose

This policy governs all employee business expenses and financial commitments made on behalf of Nexora Technologies. All expenditure must be necessary, reasonable, and properly authorised. Finance reserves the right to decline reimbursement for any expense that does not meet these criteria.

---

## §2. General Principles

§2.1 — Employees must not incur expenses on behalf of Nexora without prior approval from their line manager, except for minor incidentals below the pre-approval threshold.

§2.2 — All expenses must serve a legitimate business purpose and be documented with receipts.

§2.3 — Employees must not split expenses to circumvent approval thresholds.

---

## §3. Software and SaaS Procurement

§3.1 — Department managers have authority to approve software subscriptions and SaaS purchases under £5,000 per year without secondary approvals. This authority is delegated on the assumption that managers exercise due diligence on cost and vendor viability.

§3.2 — Purchases above £5,000 per year require sign-off from the Finance Director and the relevant VP.

§3.3 — Annual software spend by department is reviewed quarterly by Finance. Budget overruns require formal justification.

---

## §4. Travel

§4.1 — All domestic travel must be booked through the Nexora Travel Portal at least 72 hours in advance.

§4.2 — Economy class is the standard for all flights under 6 hours. Business class requires VP approval.

§4.3 — Hotel accommodation must not exceed £200 per night in London, £150 per night elsewhere in the UK, and $250 per night internationally.

§4.4 — Mileage reimbursement is paid at the HMRC advisory rate.

---

## §5. Relocation Expenses

§5.1 — All relocation expenses and allowance disbursements are discretionary and require prior written authorisation from the Finance Director before any commitment is made. The Finance Director may approve, modify, or decline relocation claims based on business justification and budget availability.

§5.2 — Standard relocation support categories include: removal costs, temporary accommodation (up to 30 days), and travel expenses for house-hunting trips.

§5.3 — Relocation claims must be submitted within 90 days of the relocation date.

---

## §6. Entertainment and Client Expenses

§6.1 — Client entertainment must be pre-approved by a VP and documented with business purpose, attendees, and total cost.

§6.2 — Gifts to clients or third parties must not exceed £50 in value and require Finance Director pre-approval.

§6.3 — Alcohol may only be expensed at client entertainment events where the client is present.

---

## §7. Expense Submission

§7.1 — All expense claims must be submitted via the Nexora Expenses System within 30 days of incurring the expense.

§7.2 — Claims submitted after 30 days will be declined unless exceptional circumstances are documented and approved by the Finance Director.

---

## §8. Receipts and Reimbursement

§8.1 — No expense reimbursement will be processed without a valid receipt or invoice from the merchant. Self-certification of expenses is strictly prohibited. This applies without exception to all expense categories.

§8.2 — Electronic receipts are accepted. Screenshots of bank transactions are not accepted as receipts.

§8.3 — Reimbursement is processed in the next available payroll cycle following Finance's approval of the expense claim.

---

## §9. Corporate Cards

§9.1 — Corporate cards are issued to employees with frequent travel or client entertainment needs. Card use is subject to all expense policy rules.

§9.2 — Unauthorised or personal use of corporate cards is a serious disciplinary matter.

---

## §10. Fraud and Misuse

§10.1 — Submitting fraudulent expense claims is gross misconduct and will result in immediate termination, repayment demand, and possible criminal prosecution.

---

*Policy Owner: Finance Director*
*Review Cycle: Annual*
